activity_labels - Links the class labels with their activity name.
features - List of all features.
X_test - Test set.
y_test - Test labels.
subject_test - Each row identifies the subject who performed the activity for each window sample. Its range is from 1 to 30.
activity_test - The activity names derived from the activity_labels table and the y_test set
X_train - Training set.
y_train - Training labels
subject_train - Each row identifies the subject who performed the activity for each window sample. Its range is from 1 to 30.
activity_train - The activity names derived from the activity_labels table and the y_train set
X_total - The combined results of the test and training sets
tidy_data - Subset of X_total that only contains columns related to mean and standard deviation values.
